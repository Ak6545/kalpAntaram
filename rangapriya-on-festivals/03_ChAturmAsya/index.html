1. Importance of c─Бturm─Бsya

Though the name c─Бturm─Бsya is familiar to many of us in the row of vratas, there is an impression that it is related to monks and some Maс╕Нi hengasaru (some women in the olden days who were very orthodox) and people belonging to the other ─А┼Ыramas need not consider it. This is a result of misunderstanding. From the view point of ┼Ы─Бstras this is a parva that should be observed by everyone. This does not belong either to the category called naimittika karma, a rite which should be performed because of a nimitta; Or a k─Бmya karma a rite which is performed only for the fulfillment of a specific desire, which need not be observed otherwise. It happens to be a nitya karma only, as it belongs to a category in which certain rituals are mandatory at some distinct times, and the blemish of sinfulness may occur if they are not observed. There is no doubt that it must be specially observed by sany─Бsis. But the ┼Ы─Бstras say that is the duty of all i.e. people belonging to all ─А┼Ыramas, to observe it to the extent possible.
